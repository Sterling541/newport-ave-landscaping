## Idea A: Pacific Northwest Craft — Rugged Elegance

**Design Movement:** Pacific Northwest Craftsman meets editorial landscape photography

**Core Principles:**
- Raw natural materials translated into digital texture (stone, wood grain, bark)
- Asymmetric editorial layouts where photography bleeds edge-to-edge
- Typographic hierarchy that feels like a premium outdoor magazine
- Earthy restraint — nothing superfluous, every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:**
Deep forest green (#1a2e1a) as the anchor, warm stone (#c4a882) as the accent, off-white linen (#f5f0e8) for backgrounds. The palette evokes Oregon's volcanic landscape and dense pines — serious, grounded, trustworthy.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Full-bleed photography panels alternating with asymmetric text columns. Services presented in a staggered masonry-style grid. Portfolio uses a horizontal scroll gallery. Navigation is minimal — a thin top bar with the logo left-anchored.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule lines in stone-gold separating sections
- Overlapping photo/text blocks where content layers over imagery
- Subtle bark/grain texture overlaid on solid color sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Slow, deliberate hover transitions (400ms ease). Images reveal with a subtle scale-up. Scroll-triggered fade-ins from below.

**Animation:**
- Hero: full-bleed video/image with text sliding in from left on load
- Section entries: staggered fade-up with 80ms delay between elements
- Service cards: image zoom on hover with overlay text fade-in

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif, bold) — for headlines, conveys craftsmanship
- Body: Source Serif 4 (readable serif) — for paragraphs, editorial feel
- Accent: Montserrat (sans, uppercase, tracked) — for labels, CTAs, nav

## Idea B: Modern Terrain — Architectural Minimalism

**Design Movement:** Swiss Grid meets landscape architecture blueprints

**Core Principles:**
- Grid-based structure with intentional rule-breaking at hero moments
- Generous whitespace as the primary design element
- Photography as the hero — UI recedes to let work speak
- Precision typography with mathematical spacing

**Color Philosophy:**
Near-white (#f8f6f2) backgrounds, charcoal (#2c2c2c) type, a single accent of sage green (#6b8f71). Inspired by architectural drawings and the high-desert palette of Central Oregon — clean, precise, modern.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Strict 12-column grid with full-width hero. Services in a 3-column card grid with large numbering. Portfolio in a 2-column asymmetric layout. Sticky side-navigation on desktop.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large numerals (01, 02, 03) as section markers
- Thin 1px borders as structural elements
- Monospaced type for data points (years, stats)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Instant, precise transitions. Hover states use border reveals rather than color fills. Cursor changes to crosshair over portfolio items.

**Animation:**
- Hero: text appears character by character
- Cards: border draws in on hover
- Page transitions: horizontal slide

**Typography System:**
- Display: DM Serif Display — elegant, architectural
- Body: DM Sans — clean, modern
- Data: Space Mono — for numbers and stats

## Idea C: Sunlit Craftsman — Warm Editorial

**Design Movement:** High-end home & garden magazine meets Pacific Northwest warmth

**Core Principles:**
- Warmth and approachability without sacrificing professionalism
- Photography-first with generous breathing room
- Handcrafted details: subtle textures, organic shapes, warm tones
- Storytelling through layout — each section feels like a magazine spread

**Color Philosophy:**
Warm cream (#faf7f2) base, deep forest (#1e3a2f) as primary, terracotta (#c4714a) as accent. The palette feels like a golden-hour Oregon afternoon — inviting, warm, premium.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Offset grid where text and images never sit in perfect alignment. Hero uses a split-screen with diagonal divider. Services use a horizontal scroll on mobile, 3-column staggered on desktop. Reviews in a large-type pull-quote format.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal section dividers (SVG clip-path)
- Circular photo crops for team/testimonial portraits
- Leaf/botanical SVG motifs as decorative accents

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Warm, organic transitions. Hover states use warm color fills. Scroll parallax on hero image.

**Animation:**
- Hero: image parallax on scroll, text fade-up
- Service cards: warm overlay on hover
- Stats: count-up animation on scroll entry

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (elegant serif) — for hero headlines
- Body: Nunito Sans — friendly, readable
- Accent: Raleway (uppercase, tracked) — for labels and CTAs

## Selected Approach: **Idea C — Sunlit Craftsman**

Warm cream base with deep forest green and terracotta accent. Cormorant Garamond for display headlines, Nunito Sans for body. Diagonal section dividers, generous whitespace, photography-first layout. This approach feels premium and welcoming — perfect for a company that transforms people's homes and outdoor spaces.
